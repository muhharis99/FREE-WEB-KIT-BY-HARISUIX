Our Heritage

A Legacy of Unparalleled Hospitality

Founded in 1998 on the pristine shores of Bali, GrandLux Hotel has built its reputation on a singular philosophy: that luxury is not merely comfort, but an art form. Our founders envisioned a retreat where Indonesian warmth meets global sophistication — and for over two decades, that vision has never wavered.

Every corridor tells a story. Every suite is a chapter. From hand-woven textiles sourced from local artisans to our award-winning culinary programme, we celebrate the richness of Indonesian culture while delivering the seamless excellence the world's most discerning guests expect.
